How long does implementation take?
4 to 6 weeks from kick-off to go-live. Simple setups with 2 locations land at 4 weeks. Complex multi-warehouse, multi-channel environments take 6. You get an exact timeline before committing to anything.
Will this break our existing NetSuite setup?
No. ALA is a native NetSuite feature, we configure what is already inside your instance. No third-party code, no new tools. Everything tested in sandbox first. Nothing touches your live instance until you sign off.
Are we dependent on Normaly after go-live?
No dependency. Team training and full documentation are standard. You will know how to update your routing rules when you add channels or locations. $119/month monitoring is available if you prefer not to manage it yourself.
